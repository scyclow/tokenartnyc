BREAKOUT AREA

TOKEN ART ONLINE

We'd like the maximize the reach of the conference by streaming + recording all of the talks. We'd also like to provide discord spaces for people to discuss the talks in real time. Would also be nice to record a bit of the conference away from the stage

TICKETING

Ticketing + Admission will be primarily managed by NFTs. Each token will have a piece of generative art associated with it.

  • Private sale: organizers will hand select an initial group of attendees to make up the base of the attendee list. This will consists primarily of artists, important community members
  • First round: maybe dutch auction? we can curb scalping at this tier by restricting token approvals (so, tokens can be transferred, but can't be listed on exchanges)
  • Price gauging/wild card round (smaller): dutch auction where anyone can buy them + sell without restrictions
  • Additionally, a batch of tickets will be reserved for staff, speakers, volunteers, sponsors, raffles,

$$$$$$$$

  • We estimate that the total costs will be in the neighbordhood of $250k
  • Selling ~500 tickets at $400 each = $200k (Same as nft.nyc on a daily basis + you get an nft)
  • Raise $100k in sponsors
  • If any meaningful $ left over, token holders vote on charity
