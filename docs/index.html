*What is an unconference?

An unconference is a participant-driven event. All topics are proposed and voted on by the attendees. The space is split into multiple breakout areas to facilitate these discussions.

As an attendee, you are free to float between discussions based on your interest. You are never in the wrong place. Whichever conversation you are having is the right one.
